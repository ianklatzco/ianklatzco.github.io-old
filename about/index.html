About

Hi! I’m Ian Klatzco.

I’m currently a full-time college student. I like computers and government, so I like to do things related to those fields. I think open source software/hardware is cool. I’m working to become a computer engineer.

My Projects page has some of the coolest stuff I’ve done. You should check it out.

I dislike the notion that technology and government are polar opposites, and I’d like to help reconcile that relationship, which is why I think things like Open Gov Hack Night and people like Cory Doctorow are really cool.

I play the piano, dance games, and Smash Bros for fun. Sometimes I put stuff on my youtube channel.

I read a lot of science fiction. Some of my favorites are Dune (Frank Herbert), Ready Player One (Ernest Cline) and The Last Question/Answer (Isaac Asimov).

I can be reached via Twitter, and you can find me on most services as iananananan or ianklatzco.
